4.3. Heat load 

a. Description

This component allows you to represent a heat load inside of your system.

  • The heatLoad impose mass flow rate at its inlet
  • The heatLoad impose both temperature and pressure at its outlet

b. Instructions

To ensure compilation, you must respect the following points :

  • Connect to the inlet of the heatLoad a block that provides temperature and pressure.
  • Connect to the outlet of the heatLoad a block that provides mass flow rate.
