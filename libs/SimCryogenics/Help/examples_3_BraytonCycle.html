3. Brayton cycle 

a. Description

The Brayton cycle is a thermodynamic cycle that describes the workings of a constant pressure heat engine.
It's composed of :
  • Two exchangers
  • One valve
  • One pipe
  • One turbine
In bigger system, you can find more than one turbine, but between each turbine, you should place a pipe to calculate the upstream and downstream pressure.
