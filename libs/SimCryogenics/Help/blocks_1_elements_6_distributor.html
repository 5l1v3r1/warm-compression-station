1.6 Distributor 

a. Description

This component is a distributor. It allows you to connect one component to several components.

  • The distributor imposes mass flow rate on its inlet
  • The distributor imposes both temperature and pressure on its outlets

b. Instructions

To ensure compilation, you must respect the following points :
  • The inlet distributor must be connected to a component that impose both pressure and temperature (such a HeatExchanger or a pipe outlet).
  • The outlet distributor must be connected to a component that impose the flow rate (such a valve inlet).
