1.7 Mixer 

a. Description

This component is a mixer. It allows the user to connect more than one component to another one.
We consider that the mixer has no mass and no gas is accumulate inside of the mixer.

  • The mixer imposes both temperature and mass flow rate on its inlets
  • The mixer imposes pressure on its inlet

b. Instructions

To ensure compilation, you must respect the following points :
  • The inlets mixer must be connected to a component that impose the pressure (such a HeatExchanger or a pipe inlet).
  • The outlet mixer must be connected to a component that imposes both flow rate and temperature (such a valve outlet).
