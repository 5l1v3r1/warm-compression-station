Fluid helium 1.0

1.1 Compressor 

a. Description

This component is an isothermal screw compressor. At each compression rotation, a constant high pressure volume of helium is absorbed.
In our case, the dynamic generated by the inertia of the components is negligible.
And there is no accumulation of gas inside of the compressor.
Finally we consider that the input temperature is the same than the output temperature, because of the oil add in the compression process and the heat exchanger water/helium present at the output

  • The compressor impose mass flow rate at its inlet
  • The compressor impose both mass flow rate and temperature at its outlet

b. Instructions

To ensure compilation, you must respect the following points :

  • Connect to the inlet of the compressor a component that impose both pressure and temperature (such a pipe outlet)
  • Connect to the outlet of the compressor a component that impose the pressure (such a pipe inlet)
  • The Kc parameter must be positive
